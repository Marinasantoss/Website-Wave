OUR STORY

***

Wave was born from the rhythmic crash of waves against the shore, capturing the essence of beach culture and the freedom of the ocean. Founded in 2001 in the heart of a coastal town, Wave quickly became synonymous with stylish, high-quality swimwear that celebrated the female form.

The brand's founder, Marina Santos, were passionate surfers and beach lovers who saw a gap in the market for swimwear that combined fashion and function. Inspired by the ocean's endless possibilities, they set out to create a line of bikinis that were as comfortable as they were stylish.

Wave's early collections were characterized by bold colors, playful prints, and a focus on sustainable materials. The brand's commitment to the environment resonated with consumers, and Wave quickly gained a dedicated following. As the years went by, Wave expanded its product line to include a variety of styles, from classic triangle tops to trendy one-pieces.

Today, Wave is a global brand with a strong presence in the swimwear market. The brand's commitment to quality, sustainability, and exclusivity has earned it a loyal following of customers who appreciate its unique blend of style and functionality. As Wave continues to evolve, one thing remains constant: its deep connection to the ocean and its passion for creating swimwear that empowers women to feel confident and beautiful.
